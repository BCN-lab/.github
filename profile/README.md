# Behavioral Cognitive Neuroscience Lab

Code, tools, documentation, and open research resources from the **Behavioral Cognitive Neuroscience (BCN) Lab** at **Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam**.

The lab is led by [Dr. Matthias Nau](https://matthiasnau.com/) and is part of the Department of Experimental and Applied Psychology and the Institute for Brain and Behavior Amsterdam.

This organization hosts code, tools, documentation, and research resources developed by the lab (**Under Construction**).

## About the lab

Our work seeks to understand the physiological mechanisms shared across visual perception, memory, and imagination, aiming to uncover general principles by which experiences emerge. For more information about the lab, ongoing research, and publications, visit:

- [Matthias Nau’s website](https://matthiasnau.com/)
- [Google Scholar profile](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=r83cbFMAAAAJ&hl=de&oi=sra)

## What you can find here
This GitHub organization contains repositories for:

- **Research code** associated with lab projects and publications
- **Reusable tools and utilities** for behavioral and neuroimaging studies
- **Documentation and manuals** for lab members and collaborators
- **Teaching and training materials** related to coding, analysis, and open science

## OpenMReye

A major open-source project from the lab is **OpenMReye**, a framework for MR-based eye tracking without a camera for research and clinical applications.

- [OpenMReye website](https://matthiasnau.com/openmreye/)
- [OpenMReye GitHub organization](https://github.com/deepmreye)
